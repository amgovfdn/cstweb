The mission of the Center for Strategic Translation (CST) is to provide statesmen and scholars with the tools needed to interpret the intentions of the Chinese party-state today while training a new generation of China specialists with the skills needed to guide our relations with the China of tomorrow.

The Center is an initiative of American Governance Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that studies and promotes the betterment of American public institutions.
