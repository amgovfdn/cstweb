The mission of the Center for Strategic Translation (CST) is to provide statesmen and scholars with the tools needed to interpret the intentions of the Chinese party-state of today while training a new generation of China specialists to guide our interactions with China over the century to come. The Center is an initiative of American Governance Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that studies and promotes the betterment of American public institutions.
