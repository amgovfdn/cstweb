The mission of the Center for Strategic Translation is to provide the statesmen and scholars of the West with the tools necessary for interpreting the intentions of the Chinese party- state while training a new generation of China specialists to guide our interactions with China over the course of this century. The Center is an initiative of American Governance Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization which studies and promotes the betterment of American public institutions.
